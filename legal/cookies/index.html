1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to store information. They help websites remember your preferences and keep you signed in. We also use localStorage (browser-based storage) for saving preferences and consent choices.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function and cannot be disabled.

Name Provider Purpose Duration
sb-*-auth-token Supabase Keeps you signed in to your account. 7 days
crc_theme CRC (localStorage) Remembers your selected theme and accent color. Persistent until cleared
crc_cookie_consent CRC (localStorage) Stores your cookie consent preferences. 1 year
cf_clearance Cloudflare Verifies you are a legitimate visitor (Turnstile challenge). 30 minutes

Legal basis: Legitimate interest — strictly necessary to provide the service.

2.2 Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics service that does not use client-side cookies, does not track individual users across sites, and does not collect personal data. It sends a beacon request from your browser and is only loaded with your consent.

Legal basis: Consent.

2.3 Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use advertising or marketing cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, social media tracking pixels, or cookies that sell or share your data with advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services may set cookies or process data when you use the Website:

  • Supabase — Authentication session cookies. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — Security-related cookies. Privacy Policy
  • Embedded content — YouTube and Twitch embeds (for run videos) may set their own cookies. We load these using privacy-enhanced modes where available. YouTube · Twitch

4. Your Choices

When you first visit the Website, a cookie consent banner lets you accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or manage specific categories. You can change your preferences at any time via Cookie Settings in the footer, or through your browser's privacy settings.

Note: Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from signing in and may affect other functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time and will update the "Last updated" date at the top. If we add new categories of non-essential cookies, we will ask for your consent again.
