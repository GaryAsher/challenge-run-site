Welcome to the Challenge Run Community! We're building a home for tracking challenge runs, deathless runs, hitless achievements, and other impressive gaming feats.

## What We Track

- **Deathless runs** - Complete a game without dying
- **Hitless runs** - Complete without taking any damage
- **No-hit runs** - The ultimate challenge
- **Game-specific challenges** - Unique challenges for each game

## How to Submit

1. Visit a game's page
2. Click "Submit a Run"
3. Fill out the form with your video proof
4. Wait for moderator review

We're excited to have you here. Happy running!
